2017 – 2018 Self employed

consulting

Consulting for IQVIA, a $10B US health data services firm in a client-facing role, I led the implementation of a de-identification service for specialty pharmacy data feeds.

  • Managed stakeholders from IQVIA, Privacy Analytics, and a counterpart (Management Science Associates).
  • Performed extensive analysis and design of the service toward HIPAA compliance, stability, completeness of data handling, stability of the production environment, and information security.
  • Led the specification of features and non-functional specs for the bespoke de-identification engine. Wrote prototype scripts for transport and de-identification.

This new line of business was established, with some 90 feeds live within the first year.

Acting as an internal consultant across operations, I worked with the management team on a variety of critical initiatives including:
